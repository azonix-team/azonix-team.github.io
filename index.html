Our Goals

We like to ensure that our mods fit the design philosophy of Minecraft. Every new friend, foe, and location should feel like a new experience for the player to enjoy.

Expectations

Explore many biomes and locations, while meeting new friends along the way. Experiment with a variety of foods and potions, build with an expanded selection of materials, and equip new gear for your next fight.
